Currency Force

Propietary algorithm which measures a currency's strength achieved during the last 30 days. The calculation traverses the different time periods (minute, hourly and daily) and provides a snap-shot of current status in the market.

Active Crosses

Garman and Klass estimator for volatility applied during the last 1 hour.

Economic Strength Estimator

Propietary algorithm which measures the level of positiveness - within a 52 weeks window - of the different announces given thru the economic calendar.
