Recommended Distribution

This is the result of applying multiple Portfolio Optimization algorithms after doing statistical analysis through historical and recent prices, plus the application of the results from studies by Nobel's Price Henry Markowitz. The risk/return ratio across all crosses in this portfolio is balanced in order to ensure profit and is calculated daily so it reflects recent market behavior.

The graphic can be read like: columns above 0 are the recommended long positions, while the columns that are positioned below -0, are the short positions. However, it is not recommended to choose a side literally: the graphic tries to tell you which pairs are more challenging and will probably give you better results if you combine them properly.
